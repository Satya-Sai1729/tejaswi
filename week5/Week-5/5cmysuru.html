Amrita Mysuru

The Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Mysuru Campus is another important branch of Amrita University, located in the heritage-rich city of Mysuru, Karnataka. - Established in 2006 as part of Amrita University. - Situated in the cultural capital of Karnataka, Mysuru, known for its palaces and educational atmosphere. - Provides a focused academic environment with modern infrastructure and serene surroundings.
