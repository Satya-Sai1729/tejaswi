Amrita Amritapuri

Amritapuri is closely associated with Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham (Amrita University) and the spiritual headquarters of Mata Amritanandamayi Devi (Amma). - Located in Vallikavu, Kerala, near the backwaters. - Spread across 80 acres with modern academic blocks, hostels, and research centers. - Houses six schools and 20+ research centers covering disciplines like engineering, business, humanities, natural sciences, and social sciences.
