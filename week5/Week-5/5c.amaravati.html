Amrita Amaravathi

The Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Amaravati Campus is one of the newest branches of Amrita University, located in Amaravati, Andhra Pradesh. - Situated in Guntur district, Amaravati, the planned capital region of Andhra Pradesh. - Established in 2022, making it one of the youngest Amrita campuses. - Designed to be a modern, research-intensive hub with strong industry connections.
