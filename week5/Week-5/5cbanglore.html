Amrita Benguluru

The Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Bengaluru Campus is one of the five campuses of Amrita University, and it’s quite distinct from Amritapuri in Kerala - Situated on Sarjapur Road, Carmelaram, Bangalore. - Spread across 50 acres with green lawns and modern infrastructure. - Well connected by road, rail, and air, making it accessible from across the city.
