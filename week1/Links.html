Amrita Viswa VidyaPeetam

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, commonly known as Amrita University, is a private, multi-campus, multi-disciplinary research university in India that is accredited with the highest possible A++ NAAC grade. It is run and managed by the Mata Amritanandamayi Math.

Vellore Institute of Technology

VIT generally refers to Vellore Institute of Technology, a private deemed university in India known for engineering and research, with campuses in Vellore, Chennai, Amravati, and Bhopal, offering diverse programs, strong placements, and global collaborations

SRM Institute of Technology

SRM primarily refers to the SRM Institute of Science and Technology (formerly SRM University), a highly-ranked private deemed university in India. In a business context, it stands for Supplier Relationship Management.
